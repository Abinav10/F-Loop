Street Freak Cars in India

The sight of a supercar is something that excites all of us and the fact that they are becoming more popular in India
gives us a chance to see one on a regular basis. Although there are not many supercar models available in the country,
there are more than a handful that can be acquired from outside the country. The brands mentioned below sell cars
that deliver every level when it comes to performance. Here are the best supercars in India.

Audi R8

The Audi R8 is one of the best-known supercars in the world thanks to its sleek recognizable design. Under the hood of the
R8 sits a 5.2L V10 petrol engine that produces 610Ps of power and 570Nm of torque. It is also mated to a 7-speed
dual-clutch S-Tronic transmission system which takes the R8 to a top speed of 330kmph and can go from 0-100kmph in
3.2 seconds
